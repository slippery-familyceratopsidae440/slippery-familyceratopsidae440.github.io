Trạm Hương - Marketing Plan for a Vietnamese Scented Candle Brand

↓ DOWNLOAD FULL PLAN

Trạm Hương (premium artisanal candles) · UEH Strategic Marketing course · 5-person team project

MY CONTRIBUTION - Ch. 2.3–2.4 Competitive & SWOT Analysis · Ch. 4.6–4.8 Campaign Tactics, Distribution & Measurement
THE GAP IN THE MARKET

The Vietnamese candle market sits split between two extremes: cheap domestic handmade candles and expensive imported brands like Yankee Candle. Neither speaks to young, urban "Urban Zen" consumers who want a product with cultural meaning. My competitive and SWOT analysis identified this as Trạm Hương's core opening: a cultural-storytelling candle brand - "hương địa danh" (scent of a place) - that neither side of the market had claimed.

Biggest risks flagged in the analysis: a brand-new name with no recognition yet, a handmade production process that's hard to scale fast, and a concept simple enough for bigger players to copy once it gains traction.

CAMPAIGN STRATEGY - PULL THEN PUSH

Pull (early phase - build demand)

  • "Hương địa danh" storytelling video series on TikTok/Instagram, one region-scent per story
  • Micro-influencer partnerships in wellness & home-decor, chosen for audience fit over reach
  • "Trạm Hương Hành Trình" pop-up scent experiences at concept stores in HCMC & Hanoi
  • Branded hashtag campaign turning customer memories into user-generated content

Push (later phase - retain & convert)

  • "Hành Trang Hương Việt" loyalty points program redeemable for limited scents
  • Seasonal gift combos timed to Tết, Valentine's, Mid-Autumn Festival
  • "Welcome 10%" first-order discount + free shipping on Website & TikTok Shop
  • In-store standees and display at concept stores as a final purchase nudge
OMNI-CHANNEL DISTRIBUTION
TikTok / Instagram / Facebook Official Website (flagship + retargeting) Shopee Mall & TikTok Shop Concept Stores (HCMC & Hanoi)

Recommended a "scent-matching" quiz tool for the website and a selective (not mass) offline rollout, so every touchpoint stays visually and narratively consistent with the brand.
